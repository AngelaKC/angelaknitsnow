Comfort Cowl

Pattern By Francicoise Danoy

This beautiful cowl is knit up using lovely baby alpaca chunky yarn. The geometric design is achieved using simple fair isle techniques. The result is a beautiful, super soft, super warm cowl sutible to wear all winter long!

Needles and Yarn

Yarn

Cascade Yarns Baby Alpaca Chunky Charcoal

Cascade Yarns Baby Alpaca Chunky Ruby

Needles

U.S. Size 9 (6mm) U.S. Size 10.5 (6.5mm)

Notes

This was a fun knit. Knit up quickly and the results were beautiful. Be careful to watch your tension, especially if you are new to colorwork!

CSCI E-12 Final Project: Angela Carey Fall 2021
