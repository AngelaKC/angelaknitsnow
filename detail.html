Trollfjord Hat

Pattern By Arne & Carlos

This Fair Isle hat and cowl set is a perfect first project for new colourwork knitters. Using just two contrasting colours of Embla from Hillesvåg Ullvarefabrikk in Norway (or any other worsted weight yarn), it’s the perfect marriage of Nordic pattern and classic design.

Needles and Yarn

Yarn

Berroco Ultra Wool 3300 Snow

O-Wool O-Wash Worster Hemlock

Needles

1 16 inch circular U.S. Size 4/3.54mm 1 16 inch circular U.S. Size 6/4 mm

DPN's U.S. Size 6/4 mm -- or -- 1 30inch circular U.S. Size 6/4 mm

Notes

This was a fun knit. Knit up quickly and the results were beautiful. Be careful to watch your tension, especially if you are new to colorwork!

CSCI E-12 Final Project: Angela Carey Fall 2021
